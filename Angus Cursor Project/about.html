Where Passion Meets Purpose

Our Founder's Journey

San Francisco Jogging Tours was born from a unique fusion of two passions: running through the stunning landscapes of the Bay Area and a deep appreciation for the rich history that makes San Francisco one of the world's most fascinating cities.

Angus, our founder, discovered early on that the best way to explore San Francisco wasn't from behind a tour bus window, but through the rhythm of his own footsteps. As he logged countless miles along the city's iconic waterfront, through historic neighborhoods, and across the magnificent Golden Gate Bridge, he realized that every route told a story.

The idea came to life when Angus combined his love of running with his passion for history. What started as personal training runs through the city's most scenic and historic areas evolved into carefully curated tours that showcase San Francisco's beauty, culture, and fascinating past‚Äîall while maintaining the perfect pace for runners of all levels.

Today, San Francisco Jogging Tours offers visitors and locals alike the opportunity to experience the City by the Bay in the most authentic way possible: on foot, with a knowledgeable guide, and at a pace that allows you to truly absorb the sights, sounds, and stories that make San Francisco special.

Our Mission

At San Francisco Jogging Tours, we believe that the best way to experience a city is to move through it, to feel its pulse, and to discover its hidden gems. We're not just running tours‚Äîwe're creating memories, sharing stories, and helping you connect with San Francisco in a way that's both physically invigorating and intellectually stimulating.

Whether you're training for your next race, visiting San Francisco for the first time, or looking to rediscover your city from a new perspective, we're here to guide you every step of the way.
